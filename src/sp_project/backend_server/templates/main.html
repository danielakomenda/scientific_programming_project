Welcome to our Web-Application

As part of our scientific programming module, our team completed a Python project that involved collecting and analyzing real-life weather and energy-generation data in Switzerland. We aimed to understand the relationship between energy-generation and weather, with the ultimate goal of predicting future energy-generation based on weather predictions.

To facilitate our analysis, we made several key assumptions.

  • Energy is only generated based to the energy-consumption, since most energy sources are difficult to store
  • Nuclear energy is more or less the same during the years, because it is difficult to switch off the nuclear power plants
  • Sustainable energy, such es wind-, solar- and river-water-energy is preferred when there is enough energy-source

In the navigation-bar, you can find graphics and interpretation about weather and energy-generation as well as a prediction fo future energy-generation based on the weather-prediction. In the tab "Extras", you can find something fun, that has nothing to do with the rest of the Project.
